About The Sims and Electronic Arts

About The Sims

The Sims is one of the most iconic life simulation game series in video game history, allowing players to create and control characters, known as Sims, as they navigate everyday life. Launched in 2000, the franchise became an instant success due to its groundbreaking gameplay that lets players shape every aspect of their Sims’ lives—from building homes to fostering relationships, pursuing careers, and fulfilling dreams.

Each new iteration of The Sims has pushed the boundaries of creativity and immersion. The sequel, The Sims 2, introduced generational gameplay, where players could experience the growth of families over time. The Sims 3 brought an open-world experience that allowed players to explore expansive neighborhoods, while The Sims 4 introduced deeper emotional interactions and more powerful building tools, letting players create rich, personal stories for their Sims.

With countless expansion packs, game packs, and custom content, The Sims has become a cultural phenomenon, adored by millions worldwide for its blend of creativity, humor, and real-life simulation. Players are empowered to design their own worlds, from dream homes to ideal communities, all while guiding their Sims through the ups and downs of life.

Electronic Arts (EA) is a global leader in interactive entertainment, known for creating popular video game franchises like *The Sims*, *FIFA*, and *Apex Legends*, while fostering innovation and inclusivity in the gaming industry.

About Electronic Arts (EA)

Electronic Arts, commonly known as EA, is a global leader in interactive entertainment. Founded in 1982, EA has built a reputation for delivering some of the industry’s most beloved and successful games across various platforms. With a mission to inspire the world to play, EA develops, publishes, and distributes games for consoles, PCs, and mobile devices, reaching millions of players across the globe.

EA is behind some of the most well-known game franchises in the industry, including The Sims, FIFA, Madden NFL, Battlefield, and Apex Legends. With a focus on innovation, EA continually pushes the boundaries of technology and storytelling to create immersive gaming experiences that engage and inspire.

As a company, EA is committed to creativity, inclusivity, and sustainability. Through their games and their actions, they aim to bring people together, challenge boundaries, and build a positive and diverse community for players. EA's dedication to environmental stewardship and social responsibility is reflected in their sustainability efforts and commitment to reducing their environmental impact.
