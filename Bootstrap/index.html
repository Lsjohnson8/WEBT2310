Sims 2

This sequel added deeper life stages (babies, toddlers, teens, adults), genetics, and the ability to see Sims age and pass through generations. It introduced more complex aspirations, personality traits, and expanded the gameplay with detailed family dynamics.

Sims 3

The Sims 3 introduced an open-world neighborhood, allowing Sims to explore their town freely without loading screens. The game emphasized customization through the "Create-a-Style" tool, which lets players customize almost everything, from furniture to clothing.

Sims 4

This installment focused on enhancing emotions and social interactions, allowing Sims to react more dynamically to situations based on their emotional state. However, The Sims 4 removed the open world it introduced more detailed building and Sim creation tools.
